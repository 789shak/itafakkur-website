Our story

Built with intention.

By one person, for the ummah — free of noise.

Syed Altaf Hussain
Founder · eDrop

iTafakkur is built and maintained by one person: Syed Altaf Hussain. It began with a frustration.

"Most Islamic apps I opened were full of ads and third-party trackers. For something sacred, that just didn't sit right with me — and third-party interventions weren't acceptable at any cost."

So he set out to build the opposite. iTafakkur runs zero ads, ships zero trackers, and takes no affiliate money. Every user — free or paying — gets the same quiet, uninterrupted space for Qur'an, hadith, and dhikr. Peace shouldn't be interrupted.

When asked how a solo builder sustains an ad-free app without conventional revenue, his answer is characteristically brief:

"Allah provides me enough, alhamdulillah."
